Mint Brownie Truffles

Description

Looking for a fun and easy treat to celebrate St. Patrick's Day? Look no further than these delightful Brownie Truffles! Perfect for little hands to help make and enjoy, these bite-sized wonders are a surefire hit at any festive gathering.

With just a handful of ingredients and simple steps, you can whip up a batch of these indulgent treats in no time. Start by mixing together rich chocolate frosting and a hint of refreshing peppermint extract. Then, fold in finely crumbled brownies until every morsel is coated in chocolaty goodness.

Once rolled into perfect spheres, these truffles take a quick dip in melted dark chocolate, adding a luxurious outer layer that will have you reaching for seconds. For an extra touch of St. Paddy's charm, consider drizzling them with vibrant mint green chocolate.

Whether you're hosting a party or simply looking to satisfy your sweet tooth, these St. Patrick's Day Brownie Truffles are guaranteed to be a hit. So gather your little leprechauns and get ready to indulge in a treat that's as easy to make as it is delicious to eat!

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups brownie crumbs (crumble brownies until fine crumbs form)
  • 1/3 cup chocolate frosting (homemade or store-bought)
  • 3/4 tsp peppermint extract
  • 5 oz dar chocolate, melted

Steps

  1. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Mix the chocolate frosting and peppermint extract.
  3. Add the brownie crumbs and mix until completely combined.
  4. Roll into balls of desired size, place on prepared cookie sheet, and refrigerate until cold, about 30 minutes.
  5. Dip in melted chocolate then refrigerate until chocolate is set.
  6. Optional: Drizzle with mint green melted chocoalte.
